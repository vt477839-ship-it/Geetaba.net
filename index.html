Our Heritage

The Tradition of Pure Taste

At Geetaba Na Khakhra, we believe in the philosophy of "સાત્વિક અને શુદ્ધ આહાર" (Sattvic and pure diet). Every khakhra is a testament to traditional Gujarati culinary arts, rolled thin and hand-roasted to perfection.

We absolutely use Zero Maida. Our khakhras are crafted using premium whole wheat flour and carefully selected authentic spices. Whether it's the comforting warmth of Jeera or the tangy punch of Chat Masala, we ensure every bite is crispy, healthy, and full of flavor.

  • 100% Vegetarian & Sattvic
  • Hand-Roasted, Not Fried
  • Made with Love (Handmade)
